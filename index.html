About

Independent security engineering, shared openly.

SSRO // Labs is built from personal passion for cybersecurity engineering, offensive security concepts, automation, and practical research.

The initiative exists to document experiments and publish useful tools, scripts, notes, and solutions for learning and community sharing.

It does not provide commercial services, client work, paid engagements, consulting, or business offers.
